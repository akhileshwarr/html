THE BAND

we love music

Imagine Dragons is an American rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada. It currently consists of lead vocalist Dan Reynolds, lead guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee, and drummer Daniel Platzman. The band first gained exposure in 2012 with the releases of their debut studio album Night Visions and the album's first single "It's Time". Billboard placed them at the top of their "Year In Rock" rankings for 2013 and named Imagine Dragons their "Breakthrough Band of 2013".
